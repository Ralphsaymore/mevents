Welcome {{profile.nickname}} , it's wonderful to see you on mEvents(Memorable Events) platform. Memorable Events platform is a professional social media network for intiating events,co-managing events and archiving information about the events you engaged in. The idea is about creating a MEMORY. We love social networking that have an life-long impact to you, your workmates, your family and your friends.
Here is how you get started

  • Go to Explore Tab and browse on the providers and services currently on this platform
  • Go to MyEvents tab, then click on Start Event to intiate a soon coming event
  • Go to Services and search for services you may like to add to your event
  • Click on the service banner to get redirected to the service page
  • Study about the service, view the images and Intro Video
  • If intrested, click on View Providerto learn more about the provider, especially feedback from other people who once received the same service from the same provider
  • If interested, click on the service again, to go to that specific service page
  • Now you can add to your wish list, or book the service
  • To Pay for your services, go to you Bookings and click on the service you want to pay, then click on the Process Payment button
  • For more, follow the Getting Started link
